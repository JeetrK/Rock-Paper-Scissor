ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple hand game where two players simultaneously form one of three shapes: rock (a fist), paper (an open hand), or scissors (two fingers). Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock; the goal is to choose the shape that defeats your opponent's choice. Click the corresponding button to your choice of play. Play best out of 3 against the computer.
